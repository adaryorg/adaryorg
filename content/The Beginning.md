Every story needs some kind of a beginning, and this is a yet another beginning for adary.org which went through a few incarnations since I got the domain some 12-odd years ago.
What (or who) is adary? Its me. Adar Y.
Many years ago I started working at a company that had a username/email policy to take the last name, and append the first letter of the first name, and adary started rolling off the tongue, and in all honesty a few people (including my wife) still call me adary. 
What will this blog be about? I still don't really know. The idea is to put some thoughts to the paper (or this new digital medium of my choosing) and just run with it. 
One thing I'm sure of is that it will feature some techy stuff, some hardware stuff, and a lot of software stuff. After all, i've been dealing with software and various kinds of hardware both professionally and as a hobbyist for the last 30-odd years (yes I know i'm old ...) 
So let's kick this one off!
